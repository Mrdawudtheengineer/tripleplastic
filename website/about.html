The Story of Triple A

Triple A was founded in Ethiopia by three brothers whose names all begin with the letter 'A'. Growing up with a shared passion for engineering and entrepreneurship, they saw a gap in the local market for high-quality, reliable industrial plastic manufacturing. What started as a small family dream has grown into one of Ethiopia's most trusted wholesale suppliers.

Local Roots

Family Integrity

Advanced Manufacturing Process

Our films are produced using high-precision blown-film and cast-film extrusion processes right here in our Ethiopian facility. Thermoplastic resins are melted and forced through a die to form a thin film. For blown-film, air is used to expand the tube of molten polymer which is then cooled and flattened to form a continuous sheet. This method provides superior strength—perfect for the demanding industrial environments our customers operate in.

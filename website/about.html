The Story of Triple A

Triple A was founded in Ethiopia by Abdurahman Omar, an engineer and entrepreneur with a vision to transform Ethiopia's plastic manufacturing landscape. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Ethiopia's most trusted wholesale suppliers, serving over 500 active partners across the country.

Founder & Vision

Abdurahman Omar

Abdurahman Omar founded Triple A Plastic Manufacturing with a singular mission: to provide Ethiopia with world-class plastic solutions manufactured locally. With over 2 years of experience in industrial manufacturing and a deep commitment to quality, Abdurahman established Triple A on principles of accountability, accuracy, and advancing Ethiopian industry. Advanced Manufacturing Process

Our films are produced using high-precision blown-film and cast-film extrusion processes right here in our Ethiopian facility. Thermoplastic resins are melted and forced through a die to form a thin film. For blown-film, air is used to expand the tube of molten polymer which is then cooled and flattened to form a continuous sheet. This method provides superior strength—perfect for the demanding industrial environments our customers operate in.

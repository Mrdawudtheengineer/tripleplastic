Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity? +

Our minimum order is 5,000 units for standard products. Larger quantities receive better pricing. Contact us for custom minimum quantities.

How long does delivery take? +

Standard orders are fulfilled within 3-5 business days. Rush orders can be completed in 24 hours with additional fees. We arrange logistics throughout Ethiopia.

Do you make custom products? +

Yes! We specialize in custom orders. We can customize color, thickness (microns), size, and specifications. Lead time for custom orders is typically 7-14 days.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept bank transfers, cash payments, and monthly invoicing for established wholesale partners. Contact our sales team for payment terms.

Are your products certified? +

Yes, we hold ISO 9001 (Quality Management), ISO 14001 (Environmental), and FDA approvals for food-contact materials. All products are 24/7 quality tested.

How can I request a sample? +

Contact us via email or phone with your specifications. We can send samples within 2-3 days. Some samples may have a small charge depending on customization.
